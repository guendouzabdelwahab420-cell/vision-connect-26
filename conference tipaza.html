Official Publication & Books

All accepted, registered, and presented high-quality papers will be compiled into formal academic volumes:

  • Springer Book Series: Selected outstanding papers will be published as archival book chapters in the prestigious Springer 'Lectures Notes in Electrical Engineering'.
  • IEEE Xplore Digital Library: Main conference proceedings will be submitted for indexing in IEEE Xplore, ensuring maximum global visibility.
  • Scopus & WoS Indexing: Past proceedings have been successfully indexed by Scopus, EI Compendex, and Web of Science.
ℹ️

Key Conference Information

Essential details for organizing committees, academic delegations, and international authors:

  • Submission Format: Full papers must follow the official template standard, strictly limited to 4–6 pages including all figures and structural references.
  • Presentation Modes: To support global inclusion, the conference features a hybrid model including both physical presentation rooms in Tipaza and live virtual streams.
  • Registration Scope: Standard registration includes access to all scientific sessions, physical conference kits, catering loops, and digital copies of the published books.
